# Customer--Retention-Revenue-Analysis

**Problem Statement**
Business often experience unstable revenue despite steady customer acquisition. This project analyzes customer retention, repeat purchase behavior, and revenue drivers to identify risks and provide actionable business recommendations.

**Objectives**
- Analyze revenue and customer trends over time
- Identify retention patterns using cohort analysis
- Measure repeat purchase behavior and churn signals
- Translate insights into strategic business recommendations

**Dashboard Overview**
1. Revenue & Customer Retention Overview
2. Customer Cohort Retention Analysis
3. Customer Behavior & Revenue Drivers
4. Business Risks & Strategic Recommendation

**Key Insights**
- Revenue volatility is driven by declining repeat purchase behavior
- Newer customer cohorts churn faster than earlier cohorts
- Purchase frequency and customer lifetime value decline over time
- Revenue is concentrated among a small set of products

**Tools Used**
- POwer BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Cohort Analysis
- Business Metrics ( CLV, Retention, Churn)

**Dataset Source : Kaggle - online Retail II**
